Microsoft SecondLight

The SecondLight combines rear projection with camera gesture tracking and uses an electronically switchable diffuser to separate two images entwined in the projection. One image appears on the surface, the other in the air above. For my masters project I was lucky enough to get access to one to write software for. Working with musicians we deployed a very unique and colourful live music controller.
